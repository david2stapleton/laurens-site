Lauren Sutherland is a soprano and voice teacher based in Chicago, Illinois. With a passion for both performance and pedagogy, she brings warmth, expertise, and musicality to everything she does.

Lauren holds a Master of Music degree and has performed extensively in opera, oratorio, and recital settings. Her credits include roles with regional opera companies and appearances on concert stages throughout the Midwest.

An advocate for accessible music education, Lauren maintains an active private voice studio and is dedicated to helping singers of all levels discover and develop their unique voices.
